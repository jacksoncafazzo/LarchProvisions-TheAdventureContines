Welcome to Larch Provisions weekly freshly prepared meal service by Emily Cafazzo & Ryan Domingo.

We cook healthy, balanced meals using as many local, organic & seasonal ingredients as possible; showcasing the bounty of the Pacific Northwest.

  1. Orders must be placed by noon on Sunday! for pickup the following Tuesday
  2. Pickup of pre-ordered meals is from 3-7pm on Tuesday at 36333 E Historic Columbia River Hwy Corbett, OR 97019
  3. We accept Cash & Check & now Paypal!
  4. Weekly emails with weekly menu options will be sent the previous Thursday
  5. If you have friends or neighbors who would be interested-- please forward this email along
  6. Please let us know all dietary restrictions in advance
  7. Meals come prepackaged in microwaveable (reusable) containers packaged per serving
  8. Consider bringing a bag if you order more than 3 servings for ease of transport
  9. We love to reuse clean containers! Just remove stickers from lids BEFORE washing or microwaving or after the dry again.
  10. *Prices below are a suggested donation. Everyone deserves access to good food
  11. Enjoy!
